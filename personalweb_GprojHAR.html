Project Details

Advisor: Associate Prof. Peng, Cong
Abstract: Human pose estimation and activity understanding are current research hot spot, with the surge in demand of video analysis, especially intelligent surveillance system for public places( such as hospitals and stations). Recently, with the development of deep learning, image processing has acquired outstanding achievements and is extended to time sequential video analysis. Though typical deep learning methods like CNN can ease blocking problem to some extent, balance or solutions of the three main problems(blocking, overlapping, large demand of computation) still remain enigma. This thesis proposes a new strategy to examine the problems by splitting human pose estimation and activity understanding to solve them separately. We estimate human pose and generate training data by Openpose which is an open source platform. We designed a neutral network and train it for activity understanding based on skeleton data. Data visualization is supported by TensorBoard. Our work improve the accuracy and robustness of the system.
Key Words: Human Pose Estimation; Activity Understanding; Deep Learning
